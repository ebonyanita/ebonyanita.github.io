[No.2] How to get to Hallstatt and back

Train & Boat

    1. Get on a train to Attnang-Puchheim.

    2. From Attnang-Pucheim take the R70 or REX70, in direction Obertraun.

    3. Get off the R[EX]70 at Hallstatt Bahnhof.

    4. Buy a return ticket for the short boat trip. Just 8 Euro (as of Nov 2025).

    5. Enjoy the scenery with your eyes, not your phone's lens.

    6. Take a return boat, and the return train back to Attnang-Pucheim.
